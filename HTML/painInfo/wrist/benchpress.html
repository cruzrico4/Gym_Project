Addressing Wrist Pain with Bench-Pressing

Advice

  • DO NOT bend your wrists when bench pressing. The gravity pulling on the bar will compress your hands into your wrists and stretch your wrists outside their normal range of motion.
  • Also, adjust your grip so that you're holding the barbell in your lower hand. Holding the bar non-parallel to your wrist will keep your wrists from bending and over-extending.
  • Make sure your forearms are completely vertical and you're not gripping the bar too narrow or wide. Having your forearms at an angle to the barbell will force your wrists to twist inwards or outwards, leading to pain.
