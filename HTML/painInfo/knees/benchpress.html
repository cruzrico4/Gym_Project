Addressing Knee Pain with Bench-Pressing

Advice

  • Keep your knees directly above your ankles and your feet flat on the floor to fully stabilize your knee while you bench press.
  • Having your knees over your ankles will prevent overextension and allow you to push through your feet without stressing tendons in your knee.
